DAZED

Ivhade can pen the darkest expression of sorrow, pain, excitement, and love. From legendary writer Ivhade F.E.O comes a sad fated novel which entails the down hill motion of a lawyer as she inevitably handles a criminal case.

Celine Jones wished she had declined the case. A story of a lackadaisical, pretty lawyer whose life turned for the worst after a gun shot sound in the law firm where she practiced. Now she was made to handle the case of the victim involved in the shooting, by the wife of the victim. Celine had no idea it was a setup. During the investigation, she fell in love with a man initially reserved to be a pawn to her advantage not minding the fact that she was engaged to her lover. At the height of the case she lost both worlds.
