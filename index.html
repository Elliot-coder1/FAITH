Osemudiamen Faith Ebhodaghe is a distinguished fiction writer from Edo State, Nigeria. Her upbringing in a middle-class neighbourhood within an extended family provided a vibrant and challenging foundation for her creative journey. Later, she relocated with her mother and sister to a diﬀerent town, shaping her perspective on life and storytelling.

Osemudiamen holds a first degree in Medical Laboratory Science with a specialisation in Chemical Pathology. Despite her scientific background, her innate passion for arts and humanities led her to refine her literary craft through courses in creative writing and psychology.

In 2019, she published her debut novel, *Dazed*, marking the inception of her professional writing career. Her creative interests span across diverse genres, including thriller, politics, religion, and romance. Inspired by real-life experiences and influenced by renowned authors such as Chimamanda Adichie and Dan Brown, Osemudiamen's works explore profound themes like morality, courage, adversity, abuse, and faith.
