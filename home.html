Osemudiamen Faith Ebhodaghe is an creative fiction writer hailing from Edo State, Nigeria. Her formative years in a dynamic middle-class neighbourhood within a large extended family shaped her creative outlook, providing a rich tapestry of experiences that influence her storytelling .

A subsequent relocation with her family to a diﬀerent town further broadened her perspectives on life and narrative expression.

Osemudiamen earned a bachelor's degree in Medical Laboratory Science, specialising in Chemical Pathology. Despite her scientific academic background, her deep-seated passion for the arts and humanities propelled her to pursue creative writing and psychology courses, honing her literary skills. In 2019, she launched her professional writing career with the publication of her debut novel, *Dazed*.
